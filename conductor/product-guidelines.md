# Product Guidelines: Building the Invisible

## Tone and Voice
- **Authoritative & Professional:** Content should be technically accurate and project a professional image.
- **Approachable & Educational:** Use clear language to explain complex topics, making them accessible to other engineers.
- **Direct & Concise:** Value the reader's time by providing high-signal information with minimal fluff.

## Visual and Design Principles
- **Clean & Minimalist:** Emphasize content readability with a clutter-free interface and consistent spacing.
- **Technical Aesthetics:** Use appropriate fonts (Source Sans Pro, Pacifico) and syntax highlighting to reflect the project's technical nature.
- **Accessible Design:** Ensure high contrast, clear headings, and responsive layouts that work seamlessly on all devices.

## Content Standards
- **High-Quality Technical Writing:** All blog posts must be thoroughly researched and peer-reviewed for accuracy.
- **Semantic Formatting:** Use proper Markdown structure, including meaningful headings and formatted code blocks.
- **Rich Media Integration:** Enhance technical explanations with relevant images, diagrams (Mermaid), and math notation (KateX).

## User Experience (UX)
- **Fast Performance:** Optimize assets and minimize page weight for quick load times.
- **Intuitive Navigation:** Users should easily find content via categorized posts, search functionality, and a clear menu.
- **Engaging Interactivity:** Provide clear feedback for interactive elements like the dark/light theme toggle and share buttons.
